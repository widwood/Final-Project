CoHive Clapham is the result of a merger between CoHive and Clapham Collective, the first ever coworking event space & private office in Medan - North Sumatra.

Although it gets busy by day, CoHive Clapham will never make you feel captivated, noting its high-ceiling, tall glasses, big tables, and sunlight-lit rooms. When meeting a client, you may book one of the meeting rooms for the privacy. The vibe will keep you productive. The freeflow beverage will keep you feeling refreshed.

Also, CoHive Clapham is a strategic place to host events. Strategically located right behind Centre Point Mall, CoHive Clapham Coworking Event Space & Private Office in Medan - North Sumatra is within walking distance from Medan Train Station, Lapangan Merdeka Kota Medan, and numerous restaurants, to mention a few: The Manhattan Fish Market, Soto Medan 1930, and Kopitiam Happy.

How to get there:

From Jl. Jawa, turn left to Jl. Veteran. Then, take another left to Komplek Center Point. On the right, you'll find CoHive Clapham just before the intersection.
