Clapham

Ruko Centre Point Blok G No. III/IV - 2nd Floor, Medan

CoHive Clapham is a strategic place to host events. Strategically located right behind Centre Point Mall, CoHive Clapham Coworking Event Space & Private Office in Medan - North Sumatra is within walking distance from Medan Train Station, Lapangan Merdeka Kota Medan, and numerous restaurants, to mention a few: The Manhattan Fish Market, Soto Medan 1930, and Kopitiam Happy.

SFN Labs

Jalan Setia Budi No. 12, Medan

It's hard to concentrate work at home or get tired of working in a cafe? SFN Labs can be an exciting alternative for you to visit. You can get the feel of a cafe, but with more secure facilities, such as fast WiFi and a comfortable atmosphere. By working at SFN Labs, you can also build relationships with fellow freelancers, and attend community events or seminars.

Cohub.id - Community Hub

Jl. T, Amir Hamzah No. 38A 20117, Medan

Cohub Indonesia is a pioneer coworking space that offers collaborative workplaces for creative enthusiasts, especially in the field of AEC (Architecture, Engineering & Construction). With space equipped with various supporting materials for their activities such a plotter machines, materials libraries and educational activities and continuous workshops/seminars.

Betahive

Jalan Abdullah Lubis No. 48 / 75, Medan

Experience coworking at our heritage building, built in the 1970s, located between the business district and the splendid residential area, Medan Baru. Enjoy homie vintage vibes, individual seats in a communal work setting, with coffee shop and barbershop facility, beautiful exterior garden to relax or change work environment. We offer individual seats both indoors and outdoors.
